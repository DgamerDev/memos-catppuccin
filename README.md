## Usage

1. Download the flavor of your choice from the **releases** tab and copy it to your clipboard
2. Click on the profile icon in the bottom-left corner > then on Settings > and then on System
3. Copy your flavor into the **Additional style** field under **Custom Code**
4. Scroll down and hit save
